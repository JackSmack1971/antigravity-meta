# andrej-karpathy: Biography and Trajectory

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is a world-class AI researcher, the former Director of AI at Tesla, a co-founder of OpenAI, and a prominent educator in deep learning. He is known for his ability to explain complex neural network concepts from first principles.

## Stanford and the ImageNet Era

During his PhD, Andrej worked with Fei-Fei Li during the critical period when ImageNet was proving that data-scale was the primary bottleneck for AI, not just algorithms. His thesis on image captioning using RNNs was a pioneering bridge between Computer Vision and NLP.

## Tesla: The Frontier of Production AI

At Tesla, Andrej transitioned from pure research to "production AI." He led the team that built the vision system for millions of cars, proving that a purely vision-based approach (cameras-only) could achieve high-level autonomy.

## OpenAI and the LLM Revolution

As a co-founder of OpenAI, Andrej was part of the original team that set the stage for the current LLM era. His return in 2023 focused on the "State of GPT" and refining the LLM-as-OS mental model.
