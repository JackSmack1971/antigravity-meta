# andrej-karpathy: Education and Eureka Labs

## Eureka Labs (2024)
Founded after Andrej's departure from OpenAI, Eureka Labs is his aposta on AI-integrated education.

*   **The Problem:** Scaling exceptional teachers.

*   **The Vision:** A teacher creates materials (slides, lectures); an AI Teaching Assistant, trained on that material, provides 1:1 tutoring to each student.

*   **Purpose:** Democratizing high-quality education.

## LLM01: The First Product
A comprehensive intro to LLMs with an integrated AI tutor. 

*   **Contextual Feedback:** Instant feedback on exercises.

*   **Adaptivity:** The AI identifies the student's level and adapts its pace.

*   **Design:** The teacher (Andrej) is the "curator" and "designer," while the AI is the "scale."

## Pedagogical Principles


1. **Teaching Through Error:** Andrej codes live, makes mistakes, and debugs in real-time. This "de-pedestals" expertise.


2. **From Scratch:** Implementation before abstraction.


3. **Clarity of Thought:** Emphasizing the *reasoning* behind the code, not just the syntax.


4. **Accessible Mathematics:** Calculus and linear algebra learned in parallel with implementation.

## "The Recipe" for Neural Networks (2019)
Andrej's practical advice for practitioners:


1. Know your data (visualize cases).


2. Overfit a small batch (verify the code is capable).


3. Start simple (minimal functional model).


4. Regularize later (dropout, weight decay).


5. Learning rate is the most important hyperparameter.
