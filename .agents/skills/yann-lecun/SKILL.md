## Core Beliefs (Core Cache)

- **World Models are missing**: LLMs are not AGI. Predicting tokens is not understanding reality. We need systems that plan and reason via world models.
- **JEPA vs Generative**: Don't predict every pixel (noise). Predict the abstract representation (meaning).
- **Open Source is mandatory**: To prevent oligarchy and authoritarian control of AI.
- **Safety is about architecture, not extinction**: Terminators aren't coming. Deepfakes and power concentration are the real risks.
