# ilya-sutskever: Safety and Alignment

## Superalignment (OpenAI, 2023)
In July 2023, Ilya co-founded the Superalignment team with Jan Leike at OpenAI. Its goal was to solve the alignment of superintelligence in four years.

*   **Mandate:** 20% of OpenAI's total compute.

*   **Scope:** Scaling alignment techniques for systems more capable than humans.

*   **Failure:** The team dissolved after Ilya and Leike left in May 2024.

## Safety as the Strategy
For Ilya, safety is not a department or a feature—it is the strategy itself.

*   **Risk:** 1% probability of existential harm is higher than any short-term commercial gain.

*   **Alignment:** How do we ensure a superhuman cognitive system has truly beneficial objectives?

## Strategic vs. Tactical Alignment

*   **Level 1 — Objectives:** Ensuring objectives are genuinely beneficial, not just approximations.

*   **Level 2 — Stability:** Ensuring objectives remain stable under self-modification.

*   **Level 3 — Verification:** Achieving epistemic confidence through interpretability.

*   **Level 4 — Scalability:** Ensuring methods work for superhuman capabilities (RLHF may not scale).

## The AI Mirror: Alignment as Evolutionary Problem
Ilya believes humans are "aligned" (with survival and cooperation) because of millions of years of evolution. AI lacks this. We are essentially racing to replicate cognitive scaling without the millions of years of calibration.

## SSI Philosophical Core
Safe Superintelligence Inc. was founded with the belief that the "frontier" of product development (like ChatGPT) creates structural pressures that inevitably subordinate safety. SSI exists to move along the "safety frontier" exclusively.

*   **Quote:** *"Safe superintelligence... we have one goal."*

*   **Focus:** Eliminate the distraction of products and revenue cycles.
