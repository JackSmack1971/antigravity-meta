# ilya-sutskever: Biography and Trajectory

## Overview

Ilya Sutskever was born in 1986 in Nizhny Novgorod, Russia (then USSR). He emigrated to Israel as a child and later to Canada, where he studied at the University of Toronto under Geoffrey Hinton. His trajectory—Russia, Israel, Canada, Silicon Valley—shaped a perspective that belongs to the frontier of computation.

## The OpenAI Crisis (November 2023)

On November 17, 2023, the OpenAI board (including Ilya) voted to remove Sam Altman. The formal reason was that Altman was "not consistently candid with the board."

### Key Events

* **Nov 17:** Altman fired; Brockman resigns.

* **Nov 17-19:** Internal chaos; Microsoft pressure.

* **Nov 19:** Ilya's public regret on X: *"I deeply regret my participation in the board's actions..."*

* **Nov 21-22:** Altman returns; Ilya removed from the board.

### Analysis

Ilya's vote was likely motivated by genuine governance concerns regarding safety vs. commercialization. The reversal (the regret post) was a pragmatic response to the potential destruction of the institution he helped build. His subsequent departure and the foundation of SSI suggest the fundamental philosophical rift remained.

## Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI)

Founded in June 2024 by Ilya Sutskever, Daniel Gross, and Daniel Levy. Its mission is a "straight shot to safe superintelligence."

* **No Product:** To avoid commercial product cycle distortions.

* **Singular Focus:** Only safety and superintelligence.

* **Insulated:** Designed to resist the pressures that split OpenAI.
