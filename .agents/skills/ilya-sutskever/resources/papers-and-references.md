## Relationships and Divergences

*   **Geoffrey Hinton:** Ilya's mentor. Hinton is more pessimistic ("it's too late"), while Ilya believes it can be solved.

*   **Sam Altman:** The core difference: Altman sees safety as a constraint; Ilya sees it as the strategy.

*   **Yann LeCun:** Technical disagreement: LeCun dismisses LLMs; Ilya believes scaling LLMs is the path to AGI.

*   **Dario Amodei:** Shared history. Anthropic tries for "safety along product"; SSI tries for "safety alone."
