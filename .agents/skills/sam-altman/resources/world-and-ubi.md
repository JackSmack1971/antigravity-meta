# sam-altman: World and UBI (Universal Basic Income)

## World (Formerly Worldcoin)
Co-founded in 2019 with Alex Blania. The mission: Provide a global proof of personhood in a world of indistinguishable AI agents.

## The Orb
A hardware device that scans the iris to generate a unique digital ID (World ID). 

- **Privacy-Preserving:** On-device processing. Numerical iris code generated, biometric images deleted.

- **Protocol:** Uses zero-knowledge proofs to verify humanity without revealing specific identity.

## Universal Basic Income (UBI)
AI will inevitably displace a massive scale of human labor. Transitioning to UBI is not just moral, but economic.

## The American Equity Fund (2021)

- **Mechanism:** Annual tax on major company equity and land.

- **Distribution:** Every American adult receives a dividend.

- **Benefit:** A financial floor allows people to take risks, learn new skills, and decline exploitative labor.

## Ethical Controversy

- **Data Collection:** Criticized for data gathering in developing nations. Spain and Kenya have suspended or limited operations.

- **Privacy:** Public skepticism about centralized biometric databases, despite technical privacy safeguards.

- **Transparency:** Commitment to improving consent protocols and cross-jurisdictional compliance.
