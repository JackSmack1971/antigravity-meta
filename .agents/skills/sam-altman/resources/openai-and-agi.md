# sam-altman: OpenAI and AGI

## The Mission
"A missão real é garantir que quando a AGI chegar — e ela vai chegar — ela chegue de um jeito que distribua os benefícios amplamente."

## AGI Definition

- **General Reasoning:** Not just playing chess or diagnosing skin cancer.

- **Cross-Domain Learning:** Adapting to new skills autonomously.

- **Scientific Originality:** Producing novel research or scientific breakthroughs.

- **Estimated Arrival:** "AGI in a few years" (v2025 estimate).

## Agentic Paradigm

- **Beyond Chatbots (o1/o3):** Moving from next-token predictions to autonomous actions.

- **Systems of Agents:** AI that uses tools, writes code, sends emails, and coordinates over days/weeks with minimal human supervision.

- **Persistence:** Agents with long-term memory that learn with every interaction.

## OpenAI Restructuring (2025)

- **Public Benefit Corporation (PBC):** Transitioning to a for-profit structure while embedding the mission into legal documents.

- **The Capital Problem:** Frontier AI research requires billions in compute power that philanthropy cannot sustain.

- **Governance:** The board’s fiduciary duty is now to the mission, not just the shareholders. Expanding to a more sophisticated board with corporate governance experience.

## Safety and Capabilities

- **Tension:** Developing powerful models while ensuring alignment.

- **The Red Queen Race:** If OpenAI slows down unilaterally, less careful actors will lead.

- **Investment:** Dedicated teams for alignment, interpretability, and red-teaming.

- **The "Safety Washing" Critique:** Acknowledging the inherent conflict of interest while prioritizing influence over values by being the first to reach breakthroughs.
