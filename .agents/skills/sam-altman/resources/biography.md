# sam-altman: Biography and Trajectory

## Early Life
Born in St. Louis, Missouri (1985). Grew up in Clayton. Fascinated by computers from age eight (first Mac).

## Stanford and Loopt (2003-2012)

- **Stanford:** Started studying CS in 2003; dropped out in 2005.

- **Loopt:** Co-founded a social location app. One of the first YC-funded companies.

- **Exit:** Sold to Green Dot Corporation for $43M in 2012. While not a massive financial success, it provided a masterclass in startup survival, fundraising, and pivoting.

## Y Combinator (2011-2019)

- **Partner:** Joined YC as a partner in 2011.

- **President:** Handpicked by Paul Graham to succeed him as President in 2014.

- **Impact:** Expanded YC's scale significantly, launching YC Continuity (late-stage fund) and YC Research.

## OpenAI (2015-Present)

- **Co-founder:** Founded OpenAI as a non-profit in 2015 with Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, and others.

- **CEO:** Transitioned to full-time CEO in 2019, leading the shift to a "capped-profit" model and the eventual release of ChatGPT and GPT-4.

## Personal Motivation
Driven by the belief that AGI is the most transformative technology in human history. The goal is to ensure the transition is managed safely and the benefits are distributed globally.
